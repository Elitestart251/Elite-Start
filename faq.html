Frequently Asked Questions

What does Elite Start Company do?
Elite Start Company assists individuals affected by investment scams by helping investigate cases and connecting each matter with a suitable lawyer for a suitable case.
How do I know if I qualify for assistance?
If you previously invested in a company or financial opportunity and experienced financial loss, you may qualify. Contact our administration team for case verification.
Do I receive a case reference number?
Yes. Once your case is registered and verified, you will receive an official Elite Start Case Reference which you may provide when communicating with our staff.
How long does the process take?
Timeframes differ depending on legal procedures and case complexity. Our team provides updates throughout the recovery process.
